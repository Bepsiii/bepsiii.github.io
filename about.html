My Goals

My primary goal is to continuously learn and grow as a developer and creator. I aim to build projects that are not only functional and efficient but also aesthetically pleasing and enjoyable to use. In the long term, I'm passionate about exploring the intersection of web technology and interactive art.

Interests & Hobbies

Beyond the screen, I have a deep interest in custom mechanical keyboards, high-fidelity audio, and PC hardware. I'm always tinkering with my setup to get the best possible experience.

This passion for hardware often inspires my digital projects, pushing me to create things that are both technically sound and beautifully designed.

What You'll Find Here

This site serves as a central hub for my work, thoughts, and discoveries. You'll find detailed write-ups of my projects, honest reviews of gear I use, and a collection of resources like spreadsheets and articles that I've found helpful.
