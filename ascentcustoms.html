AscentCustoms

Crafting the Next Generation of 3D Printed Mice.

The Mission

AscentCustoms is a passion project born from a desire to push the boundaries of personalized gaming peripherals. The core mission is to design and develop high-performance, ergonomic, and customizable 3D printed mice.

01. Lightweight

Ergonomic designs optimized for weight reduction without sacrificing structural integrity.

02. Modular

Components designed to be swapped, modified, and repaired easily.

03. Standard

Utilizes industry standard hardware (Razer Viper V2 Pro internals) for top-tier sensor performance.

Current Status

Working on the MC0, a hyper-lightweight shell.
