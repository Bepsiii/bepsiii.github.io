Featured Project

AscentCustoms

Crafting the Next Generation of 3D Printed Mice.

The Mission

AscentCustoms is a passion project born from a desire to push the boundaries of personalized gaming peripherals. The core mission is to design and develop high-performance, ergonomic, and customizable 3D printed mice tailored to individual user preferences and play styles.

The Vision

In a market often dominated by one-size-fits-all solutions, I aim to provide a truly bespoke experience. By leveraging the flexibility of 3D printing, I can iterate on designs rapidly, incorporate unique ergonomic features, and offer a level of customization that traditional manufacturing cannot easily achieve. This includes variations in shape, size, weight, button placement, and even material textures.

Key Features

  • • Ergonomic Design
  • • High-Performance Internals
  • • Modular Components
  • • Community-Driven
  • • Industry Standard Hardware

Current Status

AscentCustoms is currently in the early stages. We are actively working on the MC0, a hyper-lightweight shell based off of the Razer Viper V2 Pro Internals.
