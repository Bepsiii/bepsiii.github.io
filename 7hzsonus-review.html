Review: 7Hz Sonus

A Worthy Contender?

Disclaimer: This unit was purchased by myself.

Overview

Manufactured by 7HZ, the Sonus is a Hybrid driver IEM sporting 1BA alongside 1DD. Releasing at an MSRP of ~$60 USD, it targets the Truthear Zeros and Hexas of the world.

Sound Quality

Out of the box, the 7Hz Sonus delivers a clean but somewhat uninspiring sound. It lacks the vibrant energy found in other IEMs within its price range.

BASS
Clean, controlled, lacks punch.
MIDS
Natural, decent female vocals.
HIGHS
Smooth, non-harsh, lacks sparkle.

Conclusion

The 7Hz Sonus is a strong option in the budget IEM space, particularly for its technical performance in gaming.

Rating: 3.5 / 5
